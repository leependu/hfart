Horse Friends (s/t)

released 230126; recorded 220226

Horse Friends' long awaited self-titled album is the first release to feature three horses running in formation, namely Lucas Julia and Aaron. This improvised set was recorded in Lucas's house in San Diego on February 26, 2022 during a lull in the day during a weekend of shows, in what felt like a busy few months of shows.

Minimal editing has been performed save chopping into tracks. This album encapsulates some of the frenetic energy that powered Horse Friends "jams" in early 2022.

-annotation from AGG/Lee Pendu Februrary 2023
